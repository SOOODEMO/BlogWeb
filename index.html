His ideology was so stupid that anyone in the grade 6 should understand. But I see people saying Thanos is some amazingly written character. You then face with another issue in the story half of the lives are gone it takes off so many years after the snap. Life should basically be back to normal it makes no sense how life wouldn't be back to normal to an extent. What was up since it had no situation where your wondering the snap doesn't really matter out of a few camera angles and what they say you dont really see much. The Lion King
Very sad Movie...

I've genuinely never come out of a movie feeling so bitter, disappointed and angry. Be prepared, for two hours of disappointment! Be prepared for a sensational fail! (Spoilers and basically just anger ahead) Seriously. Every dramatic moment was rushed through. No dramatic pauses, no moments or time of realisation that simba is alive. They removed important scenes (rafiki and his stick, simba realizing how big his dads footprint was and how much he needed to grow, simba actually defending himself properly against scar in the end etc.) and dialogue (Scar, BROTHER, help me. Who are you? How do you know me? Murderer, etc. )only to replace the scenes with showing off how well they render water, or grass, or animals that add nothing to the story. The dialogue was 4th wall breaking and extra fart/poop jokes. Simba seeing his dad in the clouds? Too hard to do, just look at the clouds for a bit. Simbas big roar at the end? Mistimed and went early, ruining the suspence. Plus loss of him dramatically walking up pride rock. Pumba running from nala and it being funny? Gone. The magic of rafiki being surprised simba is alive? Gone. Well all of rafiki, gone. Funny zazu quotes? Gone. Ive got a lovely bunch of coconuts? Gone. MR.Pig joke replaced with a "be proud to be fat" joke. All the characters were stripped of their personalities or given more annoying ones. Timone is straight up a horrible per...meerkat. Pumba/nala aren't characters, theyre literally just beyonce and seth rogan. Rafiki was pointless, he taught simba nothing and really had no need to be in the film. He was bland. Zazu was not funny, he lost his sarcasm and became much too serious. Scar lost anything that made him seem clever or sinister. He was a weak mess that even the hyenas were not afraid of. The hyenas were either annoying or serious. Couldnt possibly have a mentally challenged hyena could we? Could be offensive. Simba was a mopey spoilt kid who learnt nothing. He had no personality apart from stupidity, came across like malfoy. No power, just a mummys boy. "Get away from my mother!" Came off as just sort of pathetic. Only thing it has is amazing cgi. This is much more of a rant than a review but sadly i cant wipe my memory. Go watch the original. This one isn't worth the time.
